## My Technical Writing Journey
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
My technical writing journey started in **February 2010**, with **Tata Consultancy Services**, as a **Technical Writer** in a Delivery Story Team. Tata Consultancy Services is an IT services, consulting and business solutions organization in India. I have worked on design documents, user manuals, object-oriented specification documents and collaborated on a few solution frameworks in that role till October 2014.
From **January 2015 to September 2017**, I engaged in freelance content writing and specifically focused on creating sports manuals.
My current role is a Technical Writer with experience in the Product & Delivery Team at **Bevertec CST Inc and Highgate Systems** since **May 2022**. My responsibilities include authoring and maintaining various documents such as user manuals, design documents, solution frameworks, API documents, release notes, installation guides, and training guides.
