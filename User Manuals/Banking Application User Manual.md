## 1.	Introduction
------------------------------------------------------------
The Banking Application is a user-friendly platform that allows customers to conveniently access and manage their bank accounts. This user manual provides a comprehensive guide on how to navigate the application's features and functionality effectively.
## 2.	Getting Started
-------------------------------------------------------------
2.1. System Requirements
Ensure that a compatible device (smartphone or tablet) with an up-to-date operating system is available.
2.2. Installation
Download the Banking Application from the device's app store and install it.
2.3. Account Registration
Launch the app, click on "Register," and follow the on-screen instructions to create an account.
## 3.	Login and Authentication
-----------------------------------------------------------
3.1. Username and Password
Enter the registered username and password to log into the application securely.
3.2. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Enable 2FA for an extra layer of security. One will receive a verification code via SMS or email to confirm their identity during login.
## 4.	Dashboard
----------------------------------------------------
4.1. Account Overview
The dashboard displays an overview of the linked bank accounts, including balances, recent transactions, and quick links to essential features.
4.2. Transaction History
The account's transaction history, categorized by date, type, and amount could be viewed in this section.
4.3. Balance Inquiry
Current balance of the accounts could be checked here.
## 5.	Funds Transfer
-------------------------------------------------------------
5.1. Internal Transfer
Easily transfer funds between the linked accounts within the same bank.
5.2. External Transfer
Transfer funds to other bank accounts outside the application. Provide the recipient's account details, such as account number and bank name.
5.3. Recipient Management
Manage and save recipient details for future transfers to streamline the process.
## 6.	Bill Payments
-------------------------------------------------------------------
6.1. Adding Payees
Add billers/payees to the application by providing their information, such as name, account number, and billing address.
6.2. Paying Bills
Select a payee, enter the amount to be paid, and choose the account to debit. Confirm the payment to complete the transaction.
## 7.	Deposits and Withdrawals
----------------------------------------------------------
7.1. Deposit Funds
Initiate a deposit by specifying the account to credit and the amount to be deposited. Follow any additional instructions provided, such as scanning a cheque or visiting a branch for cash deposits.
7.2. Withdraw Funds
Initiate a withdrawal by specifying the account to debit and the amount to be withdrawn. If available, choose a withdrawal method (e.g., ATM withdrawal or branch visit).
## 8.	Account Management
--------------------------------------------------------------
8.1. Personal Information
Update and manage the personal information, including contact details, address, and profile picture.
8.2. Security Settings
Enhance the account security by managing password, PIN, and biometric settings (if supported by the device being used).
8.3. Notifications and Alerts
Customize the notification preferences to receive important account updates, transaction alerts, and promotional offers.
## 9.	Support and Contact Information
---------------------------------------------------------------------
9.1. Help Desk
Access the help desk within the application for user guides, FAQs, and troubleshooting assistance.
9.2. Customer Support
Find contact information for customer support, such as phone numbers, email addresses, and live chat options.
9.3. Feedback and Suggestions
Provide feedback or suggestions to help improve the application's functionality and user experience.

A compilation of frequently asked questions and their answers related to using the Banking Application. This section addresses common queries and concerns to assist users in finding quick solutions.
